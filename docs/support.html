Common questions

How does the scoring method work? Each hole is recorded as the number of shots to reach the scoring zone (within 100 yards) plus the shots to hole out from there. Your total is the sum of the two; putts are tracked separately for stats.

How do I delete my account? Open Settings → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes your account and all data.

Does it work without signal? Yes — scoring works fully offline and syncs when you reconnect.
